# Dripify · Autumn Capsule

Dripify is a personal outfit assistant that helps you curate your daily fits and discover new style recommendations based on your wardrobe data.

---

## Features

- **Home Page**: Random pick from your dresser
- **Fresh Drops**: AI‑powered next‑outfit suggestions.
- **Dresser**: View, add, and delete your saved outfits.
- **Persistent storage**: All outfits are saved in your browser’s `localStorage` — your wardrobe stays even after closing the tab.
- **Fashionable UI**
- Primary color: white with warm autumn accents (#c28347, #d38b4b, #fef7e8).
- Typography: Playfair Display (headings) + Inter (body).
- Icons: Google Material Icons.

## How to Use

### Home Page
- **Today’s Fit** – Click *Pick for me* to get a random outfit from your dresser.
- **Fresh Drops** – Click *Refresh suggestion* to see a new AI recommendation based on your most‑used style.

### Dresser Page
- **My Wardrobe** – Displays all saved outfits with style & season tags.
- **Delete** – Click the trash icon to remove an outfit.
- **Add New Piece** – Enter a name, choose season & style, then click *Add to dresser*.
